## âœ¨ UX/UI Premium Guidelines (Najlae's Signature)
To make the Admin feel like they are driving a luxury vehicle, we implement:
- **The "Pulse" Card**: A central hero card that slightly "breathes" (opacity animation) tracking live attendance.
- **Magnetic Interactions**: Buttons feel slightly magnetic as the cursor approaches (hover intent).
- **Liquid Progress**: Instead of static bars, use liquid-fill animations for financial goals.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Hajar's Business Verdict**: Option 3 (The Proactive Assistant) yields the highest ROI for school owners by reducing decision fatigue.
